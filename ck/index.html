Our Story

Built around
a belief.

The most powerful moments in business happen around a table. We built a company to design those moments, and the infrastructure to make them scale.

Clandestino Kitchen was born from a simple conviction: the culture of food is one of the most powerful forces in business and human connection. Long before deals are signed or relationships formalised, the real work happens over a shared meal, in the intimacy of a private dining room, in the trust built course by course.

We don't just run events. We craft the conditions where the right people meet, connect, and do their best work.

Starting with our founding brand JoEs TaBLe, a social fine dining concept operating at carefully selected locations across Europe, we have grown into an international platform managing private culinary events for corporate and private clients globally, advising hospitality businesses on strategy, and building the tools the industry needs to operate more intelligently.

Our vision is global. Every market has its own food culture, its own way of gathering. We bring the framework, the network, and the craft wherever the table needs to be set.

About

Who
we
are.

Clandestino Kitchen is an international corporate hospitality and event management company. We design, package and execute high-end private culinary experiences for corporate and private clients globally, operating through our founding brand JoEs TaBLe and an expanding network of operational partners across Europe and beyond.

Our services span the full event lifecycle: venue sourcing, culinary team contracting, logistics management and client experience design. Beyond events, we operate a hospitality technology division licensing our proprietary broker management platform to independent operators worldwide, and a commercial intermediary function connecting quality food and beverage producers with hospitality partners.

Clandestino Kitchen is a company registered in the U.A.E., operating internationally with deep roots in European food culture and a client base across the US, UK, Europe and beyond.
